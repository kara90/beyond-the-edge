The Lead Engine · for agencies & operators

Why buy this if you sell to local businesses

Finding and closing local clients is the hardest, most manual part of the business. This turns it into a system that runs while you sleep.

Finding good leads eats your week

Scrolling maps, copying details, guessing who actually needs help. Hours gone before you send a single message.

Pull qualified local businesses in seconds, scored by how badly they need you.

Your outreach reads like a robot

Templated blasts get ignored or marked as spam, and one wrong move burns your sending domain for months.

Warm, human drafts per lead, sent safely with built-in caps that protect your domain.

Replies pile up and leads go cold

By the time you get back to a hot reply, they have moved on, or you double-message someone a teammate already handled.

One shared inbox with replies pre-drafted for you, and no lead ever contacted twice.

Smart lead finder

Search any city, see each business's website quality and contact info at a glance, and work only the ones worth your time.

Human outreach that lands

Personalized drafts that sound like you wrote them, with the right offer suggested per lead and a discount lever you control.

Deliverability on guard

Hard send caps, timed pauses, inbox rotation, and spam-rate monitoring keep your domain healthy so your email keeps arriving.

Smart inbox & follow-ups

Two-way conversations, pre-drafted replies you approve in a click, and a follow-up sequence you fully control.

Built for a team

Roles, task assignment with real alerts, internal chat, and multiple offices, so your whole crew works one clean pipeline.

One-click site builder

Turn a lead into a finished landing page or a rebuilt site in minutes, the perfect free gift that closes the deal.

Client Growth · for local businesses

Why buy this if you run a local business

You are busy, you have money, and you have no time to learn complicated software. This is the opposite of that: everything you need, nothing you do not, set up for you.

Every missed call is lost money

Most callers never call back, they just call the next business. Those are booked jobs walking out the door, every day.

Miss a call and the app instantly texts them back, so the lead never goes cold.

No-shows and forgotten customers

Empty slots you can't fill, and hundreds of past customers who simply drifted away and never came back.

Automatic reminders cut no-shows, and quiet customers get a nudge to come back.

Other software needs a manual

The big all-in-one tools take months to learn. No busy owner has a spare day, let alone a spare month.

Dead-simple by design, and we set the whole thing up for you.

Missed-call text-back

Never lose another caller. Miss the call, they get a friendly text in seconds, and you catch the job.

Reminders & win-backs

Automatic appointment reminders that cut no-shows, and gentle nudges that bring quiet customers back in.

More 5-star reviews

Set-and-forget birthdays & offers

Set it once, pick the gift or offer, and the app sends it on every customer's birthday, forever. No manual work.

One simple inbox

Every text and email from customers in one place, with replies drafted for you. Your assistant runs it in minutes.
