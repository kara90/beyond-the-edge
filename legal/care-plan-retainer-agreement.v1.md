BEYOND THE EDGE STUDIO
Care Plan and Retainer Agreement
This Care Plan and Retainer Agreement (the “Agreement”) is between Sebastien Vautier, professionally known as Sebastien Ricci, doing business as Beyond the Edge Studio, 4375 N Las Vegas Blvd, Suite 7 PMB 5028, Las Vegas, NV 89115, and any successor entity to which this Agreement is assigned (the “Studio”), and the client identified at checkout or on an order form (the “Client”). It governs all recurring monthly and annual plans, including Care, Presence, Growth, and App Care (each, a “Plan”). Purchased project builds are governed by the Client Services Agreement (the “CSA”); both documents can apply to the same Client.
# 1. Acceptance and Structure
The Client accepts this Agreement by checking an acceptance box at checkout, signing an order form, or paying the first Plan invoice, whichever occurs first. The Plan tier, price, billing cycle, any minimum term, and any Complimentary Build terms are stated at checkout or on the order form (the “Plan Order”), which incorporates this Agreement and controls if they conflict.
# 2. Plans and Included Services
2.1 Care, $300 per month: hosting, security, SSL, and backups for one site; technical fixes so the site stays live; up to four (4) small content updates per month (text and image changes of up to thirty minutes each); and one (1) social content piece per month.
2.2 Presence, $600 per month: everything in Care, plus four (4) social content pieces per month in total (replacing the single Care piece), including one (1) short social video; automated review request messaging; missed-call text-back configured where the Client’s phone setup supports it; one (1) content update with light optimization; and a one-page monthly performance summary. Short social videos are quick branded clips; filmed or fully produced commercials are separate add-ons. Messaging features are subject to Section 6.3.
2.3 Growth, from $1,800 per month: everything in Presence, plus ongoing content and campaign management, ongoing SEO and Google Business management, and ads creative with performance optimization, in each case as scoped on the Plan Order.
2.4 App Care, from $300 per month: hosting, updates, and security for one app; push notification management; and technical fixes.
2.5 Monthly allowances reset each billing cycle and do not roll over. Deliverables that cannot be completed because the Client does not respond or provide materials are forfeited for that month, not banked. Work beyond the included allowances is quoted at the Studio’s published add-on prices or then-current rates.
2.6 Fair use. Requests are queued and typically completed within two (2) to five (5) business days, one active request at a time for Care and Presence. Plans provide defined monthly services and are not a substitute for a dedicated employee or unlimited agency retainer.
# 3. Billing, Renewal, and Cancellation
3.1 Automatic renewal disclosure. Plans are billed in advance and renew automatically, monthly plans each month and annual plans each year at the stated annual rate, until cancelled. By purchasing a Plan, the Client authorizes the Studio and its payment processor to charge the Client’s payment method on each renewal date at the then-current rate until the Client cancels. A confirmation with these terms and cancellation instructions is sent after purchase.
3.2 Cancellation. The Client may cancel at any time, effective at the end of the current billing period, by emailing the Studio at the address on the Plan Order or replying to any Plan invoice. No pro-rata refunds are given for the remainder of a period already billed. Annual plans continue through the prepaid term and do not renew after cancellation; annual fees are non-refundable after the first thirty (30) days. Complimentary Build clients remain subject to the Minimum Term in Section 5.
3.3 Price changes take effect on at least thirty (30) days’ written notice, at the next renewal. Continued use after the effective date is acceptance. During a Complimentary Build Minimum Term, the Plan price is locked.
3.4 Failed payments. If a charge fails, the Studio may retry it. If payment is not received within seven (7) days of notice, the Studio may suspend services, including hosting. For Complimentary Builds, Section 5.7 also applies. Fees exclude taxes, which are the Client’s responsibility where applicable.
3.5 Chargebacks. A chargeback or payment reversal of properly billed Plan fees is treated as nonpayment from the date of reversal and is a material breach. The Studio may suspend services immediately and, for Complimentary Builds, apply Section 5.7.
3.6 Pause Program. A Client in good standing may pause a monthly Plan by written request, effective at the next billing date. While paused: recurring deliverables and allowances stop; any hosted site remains live at a hosting-only rate of $49 per month per site; and, for a Sponsored Site, the Minimum Term clock stops and resumes when the Plan reactivates. A Plan may be paused for up to three (3) consecutive months, and no more than twice in any twelve (12) month period. At the end of a pause the Plan resumes automatically unless cancelled under Section 3.2 (and, for a Sponsored Site within its Minimum Term, Section 5.8). Pausing does not waive amounts already due.
# 4. Purchased-Build Clients: Off-boarding
A Client that purchased its website or app under the CSA owns it per the CSA, and Plans are optional. If such a Client cancels all Plans: hosting winds down thirty (30) days after the final paid period ends; on request within that window, the Studio provides one complete export of the site files at no charge; optional migration assistance is available at $250; and after the wind-down the Studio may delete hosted copies and backups. Cancellation never affects ownership of a purchased build.
# 5. Complimentary Build Program
5.1 Offer. Where stated on the Plan Order, the Studio designs, builds, and hosts a landing page or starter website for the Client at no build fee (a “Sponsored Site”), conditioned on the Client’s enrollment in a qualifying Plan (Care at $300 per month or higher, unless the Plan Order states otherwise) and the Minimum Term. The Client acknowledges these conditions are disclosed at signup and that the Sponsored Site is provided as part of the paid Plan, not as an unconditional gift.
5.2 Minimum Term. The qualifying Plan must remain active and paid for six (6) months from the Sponsored Site’s launch, or the period stated on the Plan Order. After the Minimum Term, the Plan continues month to month and may be cancelled per Section 3.2.
5.3 Ownership and license. The Sponsored Site, including its design, code, structure, and arrangement, is and remains the exclusive property of the Studio. The Client receives a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to use and display the Sponsored Site for the Client’s own business only while the qualifying Plan is active and paid. Plan fees pay for services (hosting, maintenance, updates, and content), not for the purchase of the Sponsored Site, and no ownership transfers through payment of Plan fees. Ownership can be acquired only through the Buyout Option in Section 5.9. During a pause under Section 3.6, this license continues while the hosting-only fee is paid.
5.4 Client Materials. The Client’s own logo, images, text, and data supplied by the Client remain the Client’s property and will be returned or exported on request within thirty (30) days after termination.
5.5 Domains, listings, and accounts. The Client must register and own its domain name in its own name and account; the Studio may assist at the Client’s direction. The Studio will never withhold a Client-owned domain, the Client’s business listings, or the Client’s own accounts, regardless of any dispute.
5.6 Leads and data. Leads, form submissions, and customer data generated through the Sponsored Site belong to the Client and will be exported to the Client on request and on termination.
5.7 Nonpayment and takedown. If Plan payment fails or lapses: the Studio gives notice and a seven (7) day grace period; during suspension the Sponsored Site may be replaced with a temporary maintenance page; and if payment is not cured within fifteen (15) days of notice, the license in Section 5.3 terminates automatically and the Studio may permanently take the Sponsored Site offline and delete it. Fees accrued through termination remain due, and no refunds are owed for takedown under this Section.
5.8 Early cancellation. If the Client cancels the qualifying Plan, or the Plan terminates for the Client’s nonpayment or breach, before the Minimum Term ends, the remaining Minimum Term fees become immediately due as an early termination fee, which the parties agree is a reasonable estimate of the Studio’s loss on a build provided at no build fee, not a penalty. Alternatively, the Client may satisfy the remainder of the Minimum Term by exercising the Buyout Option. Takedown under Section 5.7 does not waive the early termination fee.
5.9 Buyout Option. While the Client’s account is in good standing, the Client may purchase the Sponsored Site at any time for the buyout price stated on the Plan Order, or if none is stated, $1,997 for a single landing page, or the then-current Liftoff price for a multi-page starter site, less a courtesy credit equal to three (3) months of Plan fees previously paid, if any. On the Studio’s receipt of the buyout payment in full, ownership of the Sponsored Site transfers to the Client on the terms of CSA Section 7 as if it were a purchased Deliverable, and any remaining Minimum Term obligation ends. The Plan then becomes optional under Section 4.
5.10 Upgrades. The Client may upgrade a Sponsored Site to a purchased build at any time; in that case the buyout or upgrade price and any credits are stated in a new Order under the CSA.
5.11 Preview pages. Before enrollment, the Studio may create and show a prospective client a concept or preview page hosted on Studio infrastructure. Previews are demonstrations only: they remain Studio property, confer no license or ownership, are not published or indexed, and may be modified or removed by the Studio at any time. A prospective client’s name, logo, and marks appearing in a preview are used solely to present the concept to that prospective client and are removed promptly if the prospect declines.

# 9. Term and Termination
9.1 This Agreement runs while any Plan is active. The Studio may terminate any Plan for convenience on thirty (30) days’ written notice, in which case any prepaid unused period is refunded pro rata and, if a Sponsored Site is within its Minimum Term, the remaining Minimum Term obligation is waived. The Studio may terminate immediately for the Client’s material breach, unlawful content or conduct, abuse of Studio personnel, or nonpayment. Sections 3 through 9 survive as needed to give effect to their terms.
9.2 Critical Technology Events. The services depend on third-party artificial intelligence, automation, hosting, and infrastructure technologies. If any such technology that is material to delivering a Plan, including websites, landing pages, hosted applications, funnels, automations, and AI-produced content delivered under a Plan, becomes unavailable or discontinued, is restricted in a way that prohibits the Studio’s use, becomes unlawful for the Studio to use, or increases in cost or changes in terms so materially that continued performance is commercially impracticable, and no reasonable substitute is available after the Studio’s commercially reasonable efforts to find one (each, a “Critical Technology Event”), the Studio may wind down or terminate affected Plans on written notice, giving as much advance notice as is reasonably practicable and at least fifteen (15) days where feasible. On such termination: the Client is refunded the unused prepaid portion of the current billing period and, for annual plans, all unused full months, pro rata; any Sponsored Site Minimum Term obligation and early termination fee are waived; hosted sites remain live through the notice period where feasible; Client Materials, exports, and lead data are provided under Sections 4, 5.4, and 5.6; and, for a Sponsored Site, the Client may exercise the Buyout Option at fifty percent (50%) of the stated Buyout Price within thirty (30) days of the notice. The refunds and deliveries described in this Section are the Client’s sole and exclusive remedy, a Critical Technology Event is not a breach of this Agreement, and Section 8.2 continues to apply.
